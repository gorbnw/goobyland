stay at goobyland!

Being on the road is magic - and it's exhausting.

As experienced road dogs we've endured the ‚Äúwhere are we parking this thing?‚ÄĚ stress spiral at 1:37am.

We know touring is equal parts dream and survival mode. For that reason we want to offer touring musicians a place to stay for free!

at a glance

  • Seattle, WA
  • Free stay for touring musicians
  • Sleeps 1-4+
  • Private guest house
  • Off-street parking
  • 15-20 minutes from most Seattle venues
  • Free music recording opportunity
  • Artist interviews
  • Promotional content creation

while you're here...

We want to provide fans with a unique insight into touring bands coming through Seattle with optional:

  • One-off, one song recording in Goobyland Studio (also free)
  • Fun, casual video band interviews for our upcoming YouTube channel (we'll send you a script of questions ahead of time)

Our aim is to provide promotional content for your music and we are determined to turn our often-overlooked corner of the country into a can't-miss tour stop!

This part is totally optional, if you just want to crash you're absolutely welcome.

chill zone between shows

We have a private, cozy 1-bedroom mother-in-law house completely separate from the main house with:

  • A very comfy queen bed (sleep is sacred!)
  • Your own full kitchen
  • Your own full bathroom
  • Living room with small couch, option for cushy foam fold-out mattress pad
  • Quiet, recharge-friendly space
  • Potential for over-flow sleeping quarters for groups of more than 4 (please inquire)

Our place is fully fenced and gated, with off-street parking.

there's a studio

Want to track a single or a cover while you're in town?

Take a snapshot of your tour-polished live show?

We run an on-site recording studio and would love to help you record something special!

If you have other accommodations and just want to record a song with us while you're here, get in touch!

More studio information available on our main page.

the fun stuff

If you're feeling social, Goobyland delivers optional:

  • Hot tub hangs
  • Karaoke party basement
  • Games!
  • Snacks / maybe dinner? (we love to cook if we have capacity!)

We also have two very charming cats - but they live only in the main house. Your space is 100% cat-free.

rage vs. rest

Want to hang and swap tour stories? Amazing. Need to disappear and recharge? Totally respected.
